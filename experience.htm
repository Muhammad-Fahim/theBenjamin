The Benjamin Experience

As you approach the doors of this 1927 landmark by famed architect Emery Roth, it's apparent you are entering a truly special place. The timeless design of the 29 floor building inspired legendary artist Georgia O'Keefe to paint it from her apartment a few blocks away. Located at the crossroads of culture and commerce in New York, The Benjamin remains a beloved Midtown fixture that continues to exude modern elegance. The luxury boutique-styled hotel excels at providing the personal attention of a neighborhood park avenue club to ensure its guests make the most of every New York minute.
