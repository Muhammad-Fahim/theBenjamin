Guest Room

The Benjamin’s standard Guest Rooms range from 250 to 275 square feet. Each is residentially styled and include a kitchenette, each with a mini refrigerator; an iPod alarm clock/radio; an HD flat-screen TV; a streamlined parsons desk with a power strip, and high-speed Internet access. Impeccably lit bathrooms are tiled in white marble, featuring a full-sized electric mirror, shower, and bathtub, stocked with complimentary bath products by Elemis.
